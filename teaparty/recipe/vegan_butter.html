Vegan Butter

 
Source
 

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup melted refined coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp lecithin

Instructions

  • Blend ingredients until smooth and lecithin granules have dissolved.
  • Refrigerate until set.
